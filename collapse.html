Film: COLLAPSE


This is an animation short film about a series of radical thinking due to lack of sense of security.
The story starts from a dream in a human brain. After experiencing a series of thrilling adventures, the people felt more and more insecure, helpless and desperate in his mind.

Finally, his sense of survival was stimulated and he became cruel. He finally lost his mind and collapsed, meanwhile the brain broke down.

The short film mainly reflected that people might run to extremes in helplessness and desperation. So we should guide and help them in our life and prevent their mind from deteriorating.

Research
I do lots of research by watching artists' work and huge amount of movie. And take several elements to construct my art style. The style change with different stages, which reflects the spiritual transformation of the consciousness.


Process
This is my first time to draw on a tablet and to use PS and AE together. It takes a long time to get familiar with the interface and catch the main keyframe of animation. Fortunatelly, it enables me to master and control the development of animation on the timeline.
