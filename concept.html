  • Concept

The theme I want to express is to make people aware of the complexity and fragility of the spiritual world and the neglect of mental health in human society. Those most in need of help are often unable to get empathy, as their spiritual activities often reach a place no one has ever experienced.

Therefore, I try my best to show the diversity of people's spiritual world in the works, including all kinds of emotions they brew in various situations, as well as their reactions.

People need to know the value of some emotional activities and human nature in order to prevent them from losing themselves in this rapidly changing world.
